Fantasy World Water Park

History

Fantasy World Water Park is located along National Highway 58 in Meerut. The water park comprises of large water slides, huge crystal clear pure water plant, rainwater dance floor, swimming pool, kids zone. The park has multilane water rides and several fountains. The water park is the best place to visit with family, picnics with kids, separate parking facilities, lockers, changing room facilities, etc. The water park also has an indulgent meal served up some delicious local and Chinese.

Interesting Facts for tourists to visit

  • The swimming pools are quite spacious and can comfortably accommodate a large number of visitors.
  • The amusement park has three lively sections of land rides, water rides and special kids rides.
  • Other attractions at Fantasy World Water Park include a turbo ride, water coaster, funnel tunnel ride, lazy river, rain dance, and a wave pool
  • Fantasy World Water Park organizes a range of water activities for both kids and adults.

Time to Visit

Fantasy World Water Park open Monday to Sunday from 10:30 am to 06:30 pm
